Privacy Policy for NBA Watchability

Last updated: July 11, 2026

NBA Watchability ("the app") does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

What the app does not do

  • No account creation, sign-in, or sign-up of any kind.
  • No collection of your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.
  • No advertising, and no advertising or analytics SDKs of any kind.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

What's stored on your device

The app saves a small set of preferences locally on your device only, using Android's standard app-preferences storage:

  • Your chosen watchability-rating weights (if you've customized them in Settings).
  • Whether you've enabled WNBA game data, and which league you last viewed.

None of this ever leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Network requests

To show you game schedules, scores, and previews, the app makes standard internet requests to:

  • The app's own backend server, to fetch NBA/WNBA/Summer League game data and pre-written game previews.
  • ESPN's public content servers, to load team logo images.

As with any internet request, your device's IP address is inherently visible to these servers as part of normal network communication — the same as visiting any website. Neither server receives your name, email, or any other identifying information, and the app itself does not log, store, or associate requests with any personal profile.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.
